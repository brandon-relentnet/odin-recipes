The TASTIEST Bolognese in ALL the lands!

Description

Bolognese is a classic Italian meat sauce that is perfect for topping a big bowl of pasta. It is a rich and hearty sauce that is full of flavor and is sure to be a hit with your friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion, carrot, celery, and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  4. Add the red wine and cook until reduced by half.
  5. Add the crushed tomatoes, milk, nutmeg, salt, and pepper and bring to a simmer.
  6. Cover and cook over low heat for 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve over your favorite pasta and enjoy!
