The BEST Cuban Sandwich you'll EVER have!

Description

The Cuban sandwich is a classic sandwich that is perfect for lunch or dinner. It is made with ham, roasted pork, Swiss cheese, pickles, mustard, and Cuban bread. It is a delicious and satisfying sandwich that is sure to be a hit with your friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf Cuban bread, cut in half lengthwise
  • 1/4 cup yellow mustard
  • 1/2 pound ham, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 pound roasted pork, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 pound Swiss cheese, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup dill pickles, thinly sliced
  • Butter, for grilling

Steps

  1. Preheat a panini press or a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Spread mustard on the bottom half of the Cuban bread.
  3. Layer the ham, roasted pork, Swiss cheese, and pickles on top of the mustard.
  4. Place the top half of the Cuban bread on top of the sandwich.
  5. Butter the outside of the Cuban bread.
  6. Place the sandwich in the panini press or skillet and cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
  7. Remove from the panini press or skillet and let cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
